Mr. Domingo Kelly "Pesed"

A man of honor, greatness, and affection towards his community and loved ones.

His Contributions to the People and his Community

  • He had been a treasurer of his village for 5 years with 2 years extension
  • Became a Barangay Kagawad for 3 years
  • Won as the Barangay Captain and served for 5 years with 2 years extension
  • Became a secretary for 2 consecutive terms equalling for a total of 6 years
  • Became then again a Barangay Captain for 3 years
  • For his last term, he ran unopposed and maintained his position as Barangay Captain for 7 years, with an extension granted due to the Covid Pandemic.
His Greatest Projects are;
  • Mabileng Payas - This project was to build an irrigation (Payas) in Mabileng to supply water to to all the lands of the people in Barangay Aguid and neighbouring Barangays.
  • Construction of the Daycare Building - To provide a safe and nurturing environment for young children during the day while their parents or guardians are at work or otherwise engaged.
  • Batukag - Uliwek Pathway -The pathway was built to facilitate easier travel for the people of Barangay Aguid, as well as residents from neighboring barangays, enabling them to access their farms with greater ease.
  • Ap-appoy - Banauwe Pathway - Was also built for easier travel going to the farms.
  • Two Waterworks in Gusod - Ili - His project served as the main source of water for the Ili, used for cooking and other essential needs. This was a particularly challenging endeavor, given that Gusod is located in a remote mountainous area, away from the Ili.
  • Water Source in Antubeng - Another source of water for the Ili for other essential needs.
  • Timbulan - Pugeo Pathway - This was a project to improve the pathway within the barangay
  • Drainage in Naidayos Served to control the flow of water, primarily excess rainwater or wastewater, to prevent flooding, erosion, and waterlogging within the area.

Mr. Domingo's Basic Information

His name is Domingo Lascangan Kelly

Born on September 20th, 1940

Didn't finish his studies and married Mrs. Dominga Suagen Kelly

Lived in Pidlisan and migrated to Aguid

Had 7 children, 5 boys, and 2 girls

Lost his wife in the year 2002 due to kidney failure
